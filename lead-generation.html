Lead grading

We will automatically calculate a grade for new prospects at the point they are generated. The grade (from A-F) is based on how much the new prospect matches our ideal customer profile (which we've already defined) which is based on a brilliant comprehensive guide from Salesforce Ben/The Drip.

As an example we will use UK financial advisers. Our ideal prospect has three simple characteristics: role, country, and the company they work for. However, we are going to use the domain of the prospect's email address to determine what company they work for, rather than having to ask them for it in a free text field (where they could also make a spelling mistake, use slightly different names, and so on).

Select our grading criteria

Using our buyer persona for UK financial advisers, our "ideal" prospect will meet all the criteria below:

Figure 1: Financial adviser ideal profile
Criteria Condition Value(s)
Role equals Financial adviser
Country equals United Kingdom
Email address contains brewin.co.uk, charles-stanley.co.uk, rathbones.com

Pardot works in a specific way, starting with a D grade by default. This will then be adjusted based on rules we create.
