We will create a fictional investment firm to allow us to work through real-life examples throughout.

Cottington Investment Management (known as Cottington) formed in 1971, currently manage around $4.5 billion in assets under management, and have offices in London and New York City. There is a small sales team in each office. [They have decided to break with convention and eliminate the channel marketing team].

Case study

For our case study, you have been hired by Cottington Investment Management as a Digital Marketing Analyst and asked to devise a digital marketing strategy. In addition, as their current setup is very out of date, the Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) wants you to work with a digital marketing agency and the internal technology team (at your discretion) to launch a brand new website and put together a new marketing technology stack to support this new strategy.
